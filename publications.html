Publication :- International Research Journal of Engineering and Technology - IRJET

Paper Title :- Rental Property System Based on Review System Based Review Analysis

Published on :- 25th December 2022

While platforms like Trivago, TripAdvisor, and Expedia offer robust hotel recommendation services, finding a similar system for local rental homes remains a challenge. Often, homeowners don't advertise their properties online, promoting them only to locals. This creates difficulties for students and others trying to find rentals near universities or other specific areas. Our project aims to bridge this gap by creating an efficient platform that benefits both property owners and tenants. This virtual system will allow homeowners to easily connect with potential tenants through a pre-arranged rental agreement, and tenants can explore nearby rental properties using graphical maps and sorted locations. The platform's goal is to simplify the rental search process, making it easier for users to find the perfect match based on their preferences. Unlike existing rental sites, this system will offer a well-organized and user-friendly solution to meet the needs of both parties.
